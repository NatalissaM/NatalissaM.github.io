Famous Pork Chops

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 4

Ingedients

Ingredients Quantity
crushed butter crackers 1 cup
garlic salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
beaten eggs 3 eggs
pork chops 4
butter 1/2 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 190 degrees C

Step 2: Heat oil

Step 3: In a shallow bowl combine crushed crackers, garlic salt and pepper; mix well. In a separate bowl beat eggs.

Step 4: Dip your pork chops in the egg batter and then in the cracker mixture. Place the pork chops in a casserole dish. Place chunks of the butter around the pork chops. Cover and bake for 45 minutes.

Lemon Cooler Cream Cake

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 24

Ingedients

Ingredients Quantity
lemon cake mix 1 package
hot water 1 cup
cold water 1 cup
lemon flavored Jell-OÂ® mix 2 packages
milk 1 cup
instant vanilla pudding mix 1 package
frozen whipped topping, thawed 1 container

Step 1: Prepare cake and bake according to package directions in a 9x13 inch baking dish. With a fork, poke holes all over top of cake.

Step 2:Combine 1 cup hot water and 1 cup cold water with one package of lemon gelatin. Stir until gelatin is dissolved, and pour mixture over cake. Chill in refrigerator until cool.

Step 3:In large bowl stir together milk, vanilla pudding mix and remaining package of lemon gelatin until powders are dissolved. Fold in whipped topping and spread mixture over cake. Refrigerate until serving.
